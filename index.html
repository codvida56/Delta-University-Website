Why Choose Delta University

Delta University for Science and Technology in Dakahlia Governorate is one of the entities of the Delta Educational Group established by Republican Decree No. 147 of 2007 as the first private university in Dakahlia Governorate. The university operates in accordance with national and international accreditation standards.
