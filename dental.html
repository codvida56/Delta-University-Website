Faculty Establishment

The Faculty of Medicine - Delta University was established by Presidential Decree No. 546 of 2019 on October 21, 2019. The Faculty of Human Medicine at Delta University is the Faculty No. 6 in the university, which was established by Presidential Decree No. 147 of 2007. The Delta University College of Medicine seeks to be a distinguished educational institution in modern medical education and scientific research at the national and regional levels.
